FIRST ASSIGNMENT

The project shall analyse and resolve this problem: make easy to buy a ticket online. This project was realized by a team who wanted to solve this problem for the projectFreedomAir. We have created a user-friendly interface to simplify the search and the purchase of the ticket. There is a simple method for the search of the flys. Every user has a personal area wher they can access with their own user ID and their own password. In this area you can search and buy the ticket or the tickets quickly and safely.
